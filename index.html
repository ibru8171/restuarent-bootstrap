ABOUT US

WE ARE PENI ICE CANDY

At Peni Ice Candy, we don't just sell frozen treats – we craft delightful memories that transport you back to your cherished school days. Our humble beginnings as a local ice candy business have grown into a heartwarming journey that has touched the lives of countless individuals throughout Kerala. With over 10 branches spread across the region, we take pride in being a part of your sweetest moments. Remember the thrill of hearing the ice cream vendor's bell ringing as you rushed out of your classroom during the hot school days? Our flavors are designed to bring back those very moments! From classics like mango, orange, and cola, to creamy indulgences like vanilla milk and chocolate cream, our diverse range of flavors promises to cater to every palate. Each bite is a trip down memory lane, a burst of taste that warms your heart. extravaganza that is unique and distinct












OUR PRODUCTS



PENI ICE CANDY is synonymous with affordability. We believe that reliving your school days shouldn't come at a high cost. That's why we offer our ice candies at incredibly low prices, making it accessible to everyone without compromising on quality. We use only the finest ingredients to create a symphony of flavors that dance on your taste buds, whether you're savoring our water-based delights or indulging in our rich milk and cream options.
